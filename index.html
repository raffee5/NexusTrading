Frequently Asked Questions

Platform guidelines, payout structures, and support information.

How long does it take to receive my returns?

It typically takes around 5-14 days to get the traded money back to your crypto account, depending on the market graphs of the specific crypto asset. You can track live market data on CoinMarketCap.

Where do my withdrawals go?

For strict security measures, all funds and generated yields are automatically sent directly back to the exact crypto account they were originally sent from.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a strict 2-day money-back guarantee on your initial deposit if you choose to cancel before trades are executed.

How do I track my active investments?

You will receive an automated email notification every time our traders buy or sell a coin using your invested capital.

How does the access subscription work?

The terminal access subscription must be renewed every month to maintain active fund routing and dashboard capabilities.

How can I contact support?

Our support team is available 24/7. You can use the "SUPPORT" button at the top of the terminal to open a secure ticket, or email us directly at nexustrading@gmail.com.
