What is a trade?

A trade is basically when one person buys something and another person sells it at the same price. You pick an asset, like a stock or crypto, and choose when to buy or sell. If your order matches with someone else’s order, the platform completes the trade and the asset moves to the buyer while the money goes to the seller. You make money if the price goes up after you buy and you sell it for more, but you lose money if the price drops and you sell for less.
